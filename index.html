Who I Am

About

I'm a full-stack developer and IT professional with a background spanning web development, systems administration, and network infrastructure. I've worked across help desks, freelance engagements, and client software projects — giving me a practical understanding of what technology needs to actually accomplish in a business context.

Before tech, I spent years in the weighing and measurement industry doing field calibration work. That's where I developed my troubleshooting instincts, my obsession with accuracy, and a clear-eyed understanding that real-world systems are far messier than any textbook suggests. That foundation shapes how I approach every technical problem today.

I care about building things that work reliably, not just things that look good in a demo. Currently deepening expertise in cybersecurity alongside active client work and personal infrastructure projects.
