No Surprises

The fine print, up front

  • → The 48-hour guarantee: if the site is not live within 48 hours of a completed kickoff form, the build fee is refunded in full and the site is finished anyway.
  • → The clock starts at kickoff completion, not at payment - so you are never penalized for needing a few days to gather content.
  • → "Live" means publicly reachable: your site is deployed and browsable at a preview URL within the 48 hours, and connected to your custom domain the moment your registrar's DNS propagates - usually minutes, occasionally longer for some registrars.
  • → Hosting is free: the site is deployed to GitHub Pages with SSL - the same infrastructure this site runs on. You register the domain (typically ~$12/year, setup help included), and the repository transfers to your own account on request.
  • → You own the finished site outright - code, content, domain, and hosting account. No retainers required, no lock-in.
  • → Ongoing maintenance is available month-to-month at $65–$325/mo if you want it - entirely optional.
